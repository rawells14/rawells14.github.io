Projects

Gpa++

Gpa++ was an early client side application written in Java, created in the summer of 2013 for students to calculate their gpa. Throughout the school year of 2013-2014, hundreds of students utilized this tool to speed up their GPA calculation process. Although this project is no long in development and has been successed by its child, GetMyGpa, it is still used by countless students.

GetMyGpa

GetMyGpa is a simple yet functional, single-page web-application used by approximately 100 students per month to calculate their High School GPA in an easy to read to format. Check out GetMyGpa.com or visit the codebase here

Panther Den Attendance and Assignation System
