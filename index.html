Gpa++

Gpa++ was an early client side application created in the summer of 2013 for students to calculate their gpa. Throughout the school year of 2013-2014, hundreds of students utilized this tool to speed up their gpa calculation process. This project is no long in development as it has been successed by its child, GetMyGpa.
